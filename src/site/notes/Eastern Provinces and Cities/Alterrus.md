Capital of the east  
  
Home of [[Groups and Associations/The EmberGuard\|The EmberGuard]]  
  
---  
  
This city lies underneath the sweltering heat of the eastern sun.  
its entrance is entirely traced by gold and silver, with Emberguard at all cardinal directions. Raw magic pours from the gaping opening in the earth, and gravity is reduced when moving above the great entry, allowing newcomers to easily float down all the way to the lowest level. This city is built vertically, with buildings and great factories jutting out from the surrounding rock, with bridges crisscrossing the gap between. the lowest levels have precious little light, and poverty runs rampant, with low-levels robbing the wealthier higher-levels.
