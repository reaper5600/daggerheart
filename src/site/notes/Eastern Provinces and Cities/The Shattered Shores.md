Sheer cliffs drop off into crashing waves below. The Earth here is cracked into large sections, with large gaps in between which flood during rare rain events.   
  
During monthly rain events, dubbed [[Objects and such/The Landfall\|The Landfall]] , rare amphibious creatures with large gills will emerge to catch rainwater to store for later life.
