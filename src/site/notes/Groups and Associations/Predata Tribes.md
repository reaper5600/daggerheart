Expensive to buy trust, reasonable to buy muscle. These Tribes live in small groups and live in the [[Western Provinces and Cities/Predata Territory\|Predata Territory]]. These tribes value strength, dexterity, and cunning. The small groups are very tight-knit; each serving as a large family.
