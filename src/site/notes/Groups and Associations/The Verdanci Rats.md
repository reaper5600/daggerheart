Act as information gatherers for Garfunkle, are payed with food and shelter from him. they often pose as servants or simple street rats to hear conversations nobody else could.

---

Reside in only [[Western Provinces and Cities/Verdanci\|Verdanci]], and are reluctant to leave its bounds.
