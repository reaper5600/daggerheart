The party begins separated, within the wandering willow 
    People wander around, order drinks, and enjoy themselves 

As midnight arrives, a large group of people (around 30) come out from the back to close all windows 

    The windows shine a purple glow behind the shutters and shake aggressively 

    After they stop, the party realize the tavern has moved 

    A board above the door flips, and shows Sagea (Sagea is in the east) 

Leora exits the tavern, and sees a dense, glowing jungle 

    Inside, a bell tolls as a door forms within the wall 

    A dracona woman walks through the door, and puts up job postings 

    She then re-enters the door 

    Mudfoot looks at a job posting. It says “party needed-lost patron” 

    Everyone but the clanks touch the job posting, and vanish 

    After convincing within the office, the clanks decide to touch the posting 

    The party finds themself within a conference room demi-plane, where they can discuss the job 

A lady named Alegarde introduces herself, giving information about the job 

    A patron has been lost 

    In order to find it, the party must enter a different realm 

    She snaps her fingers, and everyone gets a golden ring attached to their fingers, signifying they are within an adventuring party (They cannot be removed) 

    As Alegarde snaps her fingers again, the party teleports back into the wandering widow 

The party must head to Xara’s rest, the home of Celene 

    The party ventures through the lush jungle, and find thick trees 

    From a center tree, a Simiah named Ed emerges and allows the party passage 

    The tree is Xara’s rest 

    At the top of the tree, there is a large platform 

    Students venture about, people are studying hard 

Celene finds her mentor/teacher, Fennel 

    Underneath this room, is an infinite library 

    Fennel is a Katari with bright coloring, she is the god of knowledge 

    She resembles an owl, and becomes more owl-like when she uses her powers 

    Fennel shows the party to the warlock who has lost his patron 

    His name in Shinfu 

    Shinfu tells the party that they must go to the Candlebright carnival, in which they can find a passage to a separate plane of existence 

    In this other plane, they can find Crawgway (The patron) 

The party heads back to Fennel 

    Fennel opts to show the way to the carnival 

    However, the carnival will disappear in 2 days 

    She opens a portal beneath the party 

The portal teleports them 2000 meters above the sky 

    The party braces for impact before they hit the ground 

    Everyone sighs, as they realize everything is safe and they appear in the carnival 

    They are floating in the air, almost as if they are swimming 

Frayle enters a tall, orange tent 

    He sees five enormous people, including the barkeep 

    They participate in the “little chef” event, to cook a meal 

    Fides ends up winning the event, earning himself a silver token 

The party then enters another tent, with many bowling pins stacked on the walls.
